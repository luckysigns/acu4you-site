About Hilarey

Five Element acupuncturist, caring for Portland — body, mind, and spirit.

A whole-person practice

Hilarey F. Boly practices Five Element acupuncture in Portland, Oregon. Her work begins from a simple conviction: you are not a collection of symptoms, but a whole person — and true healing addresses the root of imbalance, not just its signals.

Sessions begin with a conversation — the highs and lows since your last visit — followed by pulse reading across the physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual levels. From there Hilarey selects the points for your treatment, and she’s always happy to explain what each point is chosen to do.

Clients describe her as kind, fully present, and calming — read their words here.
