The Philosophy

Elemental balance

Five Element Acupuncture looks to nature’s own elements — wood, fire, earth, metal, and water — and recognizes all five living within each of us. When one element is pushed out of balance, the body asks another to carry the extra load. Left too long, that quiet overworking wears the whole system down, and illness eventually follows.

Whether it shows up physically, emotionally, mentally, or spiritually, illness is really an indicator — the body’s way of asking for help. Treatment works with the natural creation and control cycles of the elements to restore balance on every level, addressing the root of imbalance rather than chasing symptoms.

Even in good health, each of us favors one element. The art is living in balance within your own nature.
