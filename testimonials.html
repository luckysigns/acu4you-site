I was a relative first-timer with acupuncture. I didn’t really know what to expect and was looking for relief from trouble spots in my neck, shoulders, feet, and legs. I’ve been working with Hilarey for about a year now and have chosen to continue every few weeks.

She suggests a course of treatment and offers thoughtful descriptions of her intuitive treatment points and what they’re designed to accomplish. Her voice is calming and deliberate; very relaxing to listen to.

Over time I’ve noticed big improvements in my overall wellness and attitude. She is easy to talk with, fun to know, and I trust her. In my opinion she is in exactly the right line of work, and more people should benefit from her knowledge, skills, and natural gifts.

She makes the whole experience personal and tailored to your needs.

I had a lifelong, withering fear of speaking in front of groups. I don’t know why I thought a yoga teacher training would fix it — I was assured the fear would be gone by the end, but it wasn’t. After one unkind piece of feedback, I resolved to stop teaching altogether and just practice.

About a year later, Hilarey gave me a treatment — just three needles. The next day she asked how I felt, and I realized it was time to face that fear. That very day I walked into a yoga studio I’d never visited, signed in as a teacher, and the owner said, “You’re a teacher? We need teachers.” She put me on the schedule then and there.

It was shaky for the first three weeks — then something clicked, and the fear was gone. That was more than four years ago. I’m still teaching, and I credit Hilarey’s treatment with the ability to do so.
